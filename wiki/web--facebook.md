# Facebook


## FAQ

### How do I move photos between albums?



1. Browse to your **My Photos** page.
1. Click on the album which contains the photos you wish to move.
1. At the top of the album directly beside the total number of photos in the album you will see **Edit Photos**
1. This brings you to the **Edit Album** page where you can:
1. Add comments to all of your photos
1. Change the Album Cover photo
1. Delete photos
1. Move photos to another album
1. Once completed click **Save Changes**
